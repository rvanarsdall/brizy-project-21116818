Our story begins the way many stories do...


The year was 2023, the month was March.

The time was right for a change,

John decided to sign up for an intense 3-month Software Development Bootcamp.

Up until that time; he had never coded in his life...

He didn't know the function of a function().

His style may have had class but it never had ID.

The only tags he ever looked at were the ones that said "Machine Wash Safe."


After the conclusion of his Bootcamp a whole new world opened up for John. His new skills have provided him with many unique opportunities from the most unexpected places.

Since then, he has been working on Freelance commissions, been a TA for new groups of future coders, and has been involved in programing a start-up company's phone app!


Thus, John was reborn into the world of Software Development!

Using his incredibly-average abilities and generic blandness,

This ridiculously normal and unassuming individual,

Has dedicated his life to above-average achievement,

Despite his aggressive and all-consuming mundanity!


If you want John to help guide you on your coding adventure then sign up for a one-on-one tutor session. John promises to make this learning experience not only informative but also fun!
