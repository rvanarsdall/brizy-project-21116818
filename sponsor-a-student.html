Help Our Students

Our goal is to empower students to learn more for less. For only $50 your contribution will help sponsor a student to work on a website project for you that involves HTML/CSS and JavaScript. This money goes directly towards the student's tuition account. If you choose to accept the final project that they create for you it will result in a one time payment of $200 to use it. This goes towards their program cost reducing the financial responsibility on the student.

As the students progress in our program they want to work on a real-world project. This helps them meet with you and get creative ideas to help make your vision a possibility.

Your initial sponsorship of $50 will directly support our students that work on your project. You will get a final website and if you choose to accept the final product to host yourself it's just a one time fee of $200. The initial $50 is non-refundable once we accept your project idea.
